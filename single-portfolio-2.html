Knob Hills Trail Race for Denton Area Running Club

Overview- The Knob Hills Trail Race is a marathon and ultra distance trail race hosted by the Denton Area Running Club annually in January. I volunteered to create a responsive and fresh website for them in January of 2018.

Objectives- Structurally I wanted to streamline the information that was provided for runners so that they could quickly make an informed decision about racing at Knob Hills. I interviewed a few runners who gave me feedback about what information is most important when signing up for a race and used that information as the basis for my design.

Style Concepts- I began by putting together a style concepts page that was based off the color-scheme from their logo. I also put together a mockup of the landing page with the included button example.
