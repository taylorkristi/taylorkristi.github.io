Denton Area Running Club (Concept)

Overview- Denton Area Running Club is a local non-profit trail and road running club. They are currently hosting their site through SquareSpace, and I wanted to try to build my skillset by redesigning their site from the bottom up.

Objectives- I felt it necssary to restructure the site to contain fewer menu items to click through with a large emphasis on making their site more responsive and mobile friendly.

Requirements- I also wanted to add a shop page, as well as a more information about the club's activities up front instead of hidden on numerous pages.

Style Concepts- I began by putting together a style concepts page that was based off the color-scheme from their logo. I also put together a mockup of the landing page with the included button example.
